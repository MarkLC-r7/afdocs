SSL is automatically enabled on all apps with default domain names, i.e. domain names that end in the following: 

* \*.aws.af.cm
* \*.ap01.aws.af.cm
* \*.eu01.aws.af.cm
* \*.rs.af.cm
* \*.hp.af.cm
